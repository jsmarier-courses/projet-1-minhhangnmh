Hi, this is Minh Hang!

Welcome to my playground 😁

I am a fourth-year Communication at University of Ottawa. As a native of Vietnam, I have the unique advantage of being fluent in both English and French, which allows me to navigate diverse linguistic landscapes with ease. My academic journey has been an enriching one, and I'm proud to be on the cusp of completing my degree.

In terms of professional experience, my time as a PR intern at Rose PR has been nothing short of exhilarating. I've discovered a genuine passion for crafting impactful press releases and press materials. Pitching stories to journalists and influencers has become second nature to me, and it's been immensely rewarding to see my work featured in numerous reputable Canadian outlets.

Outside of my professional life, I'm an avid cook and food photographer. I find joy in experimenting with different cuisines and capturing their essence through the lens. Additionally, I'm an ardent reader with a penchant for behavioral psychology and sociology.

As you step into my world, I hope you find as much enjoyment in exploring my passions as I do. Welcome to my playground of experiences and interests!
